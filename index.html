Making Tea

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add tea leaves and Sugar.
  3. Let it simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Strain the tea into a cup and add milk if desired.

Making Paratha add Aloo Curry

  1. Knead the dough for paratha and let it rest.
  2. Roll out the dough and cook on a hot griddle with oil or ghee.
  3. For aloo curry, onions, tomatoes, and spices in a pan.
  4. Add boiled potatoes and cook until the curry thicknes.

Making Biryani

  1. Marinate the meat or vagetables with spices and yogurt.
  2. Cook basmati rice unit it's 70% done.
  3. Layer the marinated meat vegetarian and rice in a pot.
  4. Cook on low heat(dum) until fully cooked.
